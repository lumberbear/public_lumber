Shorthand format

Type one chunk per set, separated by commas. Each chunk is SETS x REPS @ RESISTANCE.

2x50@40, 1x40@10
  • 2x50@40 — 2 sets of 50 reps at 40
  • 1x40@10 — 1 set of 40 reps at 10

Resistance can be text — try 3x10@blue band or 4x8@bodyweight. The @ resistance part is optional.
